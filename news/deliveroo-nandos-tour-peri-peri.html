NANDO’S JOINS DELIVEROO FOR NATIONWIDE DELIVERY ROLLOUT

  • 11 September 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo nandos
Share this article
  • Nando's officially launches on Deliveroo with 360 restaurants available on the app from 11th September
  • Deliveroo riders launch Tour de PERi-PERi, visiting key UK cities, to celebrate the nationwide roll out by delivering dreams of PERi-PERi to Nando's lovers
  • Prizes worth £15,000 in Deliveroo credit on Nando's to be won by people who spot the Deliveroo rider fleet

Deliveroo today announces a nationwide rollout of Nando's with 360 restaurants now available on the food delivery platform.

Nando's, famed for their legendary flame-grilled PERi-PERi chicken, is finally available to Deliveroo customers across the country to enjoy in the comfort of their home.

To celebrate the launch of Nando's on the platform, Deliveroo announces the first ever national rider tour delivering dreams of PERi-PERi to locals from Glasgow to Brighton.

Deliveroo's Tour de PERi-PERi rider team will be visiting five major cities across the UK, giving customers who spot them the chance to win prizes worth £15,000 in Deliveroo credit to spend on, you guessed it, Nando's.

The Tour de PERi-PERi will visit Glasgow, Manchester, Bristol, London and Brighton throughout September and October with confirmed visit dates to be announced on Deliveroo social channels.  The Deliveroo rider team will be kitted out in full racing gear and race labels making them easy to spot.  

Three lucky people in each city will win £1,000 of Deliveroo credit if they snap and share a picture of the Deliveroo Tour de PERi-PERi fleet on social media, tagging @Deliveroo and @NandosUK.

Arabella Jenkins at Deliveroo says: "Deliveroo joining forces with Nando's is a PERi-PERi dream come true.

What better way to mark the occasion than with our rider network hitting the road to spread the news? Our Tour de PERi-PERi rider team will be making customers' dreams come true with the Deliveroo credit prizes up for grabs if you spot them."

About Deliveroo

Deliveroo is an award-winning delivery service founded in 2013 by William Shu and Greg Orlowski. Deliveroo works with over 80,000 best-loved restaurants and takeaways, as well as 60,000 riders to provide the best food delivery experience in the world.  Deliveroo is headquartered in London, with over 2,500 employees in offices around the globe.

Deliveroo operates in over 500 towns and cities across 12 markets, including Australia, Belgium, France, Hong Kong, Italy, Ireland, Netherlands, Singapore, Spain, United Arab Emirates, Kuwait and the United Kingdom.

About Nando's 

Nando's is best known for its legendary and delicious flame grilled PERi-PERi chicken. At Nando's, we surround our customers with warmth, hospitality and fun, whilst offering them a meal to remember. 

Served on, or off the bone, in a wrap, burger or pitta, and served with a delicious selection of sides, salads, appetisers and sharing platters, Nando's offers great variety, and mouth-watering recipes to please all food palettes. You'll find yourself spoilt for choice! 

Whether you go for a quick bite, a family party, a fun night out with friends, it won't be just your taste buds that are invigorated.
