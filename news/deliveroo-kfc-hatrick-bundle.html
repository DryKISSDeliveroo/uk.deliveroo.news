EXCLUSIVE KFC ‘HAT TRICK’ BUNDLE ON DELIVEROO WILL SAVE YOU 35%

  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min

The bundle includes eight mini fillets, a large popcorn chicken and sweet chilli bites for JUST £15 (over 35% off), only available on Deliveroo

3rd August, UK. Back of the net! To kick off the football season, Deliveoo has teamed up with KFC to offer an exclusive finger-lickin' chicken bundle for football fans across the nation. New and exclusive to Deliveroo customers only, the KFC 'Hat Trick' bundle includes eight mini fillets, a large popcorn chicken and sweet chilli bites for just £15 (usually  £24.90), saving over 35% - score!

If bought separately the items would come to a total of: £24.90. The bundle cost on Deliveroo is only £15!

  • Eight mini fillets - £15.92
  • Large popcorn chicken - £5.99
  • Sweet chilli bites - £2.99

The offer runs from 6th August 2022 to 28th May 2023, meaning football fans will never miss a moment of sporting action when they order the super money-saving bundle direct to their doors via Deliveroo!

Aisha Jefferson, Deliveroo said: "We're delighted to be offering our customers the chance to bag a great deal with the KFC 'Hat Trick' Bundle that is exclusive to Deliveroo. Not only will it save our customers money but it might even bring some luck to the games - bring on the hat tricks on-an-off the pitch!"

Customers can order food on Deliveroo today and place orders directly through the Deliveroo app, or online. New customers can download the app here. App link will direct people to their device's app store.
