DELIVEROO PARTNERS WITH OVER 700 HALAL RESTAURANTS ACROSS THE UK

  • 7 May 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo ramadan promotion
Share this article
  • Deliveroo is working with over 700 of restaurants in 44 cities to offer 20% off local halal meals at Iftar from 6pm to midnight for the duration of Ramadan*
  • German Doner Kebab, Wok to Walk, BIRD, Chaiiwala, Dixy Chicken, Baskin Robins and Taco Bell are some of the Halal restaurants participating in the offer
  • Morrisons has extended its Deliveroo menu to offer speciality goods so that Muslim households can remain well-equipped to cook their own Ramadan feasts

London, 16 April: Deliveroo has today announced that during Ramadan, hundreds of restaurants across the UK will offer a special 20% off discount 7pm to 10pm for the duration of Ramadan to help families open the fast. This will be particularly beneficial for the vulnerable and isolated who are unable to shop for their Iftar and Suhoor meals over the coming weeks.

Between 12th April and 15th May, 700 Deliveroo restaurants are offering a 20% discount at Iftar from 7pm to 10pm every day, to provide meals for families fasting at home. All deliveries will be contact-free to ensure safety.

This will be the second year Ramadan has taken place under lockdown restrictions, where many Muslim households will find themselves celebrating Ramadan at home without friends and family.

Customers will be able to search for participating restaurants under the tag 'Ramadan 2021' on the Deliveroo app. Restaurants offering the discount include German Doner Kebab, Wok to Walk, Sam's Chicken, Chaiiwala, Dixy Chicken, Baskin Robins and Taco Bell. (For a full list of restaurants offering Ramadan discounts, please see notes to editor**).

Morrisons has also extended its Deliveroo menu to offer speciality goods, so that Muslim households can remain well-equipped to cook their own Ramadan feasts.

Morrison's offerings includes Shazans Masala Fries 600g, Jahan Peri Peri Chicken Strips 500g, Taj Ginger and Garlic Mix 400g, Original Paratha 400g, Clay-Oven Bakery Naan 300g, KTC Chickpeas 400g, Rubicon Still Mango Deluxe 1L, KTC Gram Flour 2kg, Laila Piri Prir Sauce 1L, Rubicon Mango 2L, Shazam Peri Peri Chicken Platter and Lan Farm Buo W/M Yoghurt 2kg.

The special offer will be available for customer in the following 44 towns and cities across the UK, only where Deliveroo currently provides coverage:

London, Manchester, Oxford, Sheffield, Newcastle, Southampton, Windsor, Stevenage, Birmingham, Cardiff, Maidstone, Milton Keynes, Windsor, Bournemouth, Oldham, Rotherham, Bradford, Leeds, Liverpool, York, Doncaster, Glasgow, Northampton, Hull, Leicester, Woking, High Wycombe, Southampton, Bristol, Coventry, Brighton, Edinburgh, Royal Tunbridge Wells, Southend-on-Sea, Bracknell, Bishops Stortford, Camberley, Loughborough, Norwich, Peterborough, Blackburn, Gravesend, Preston and Rochdale.

Sam Chandrasinghe, Managing Director, Founder of Sam's Chicken said, "Sam's Chicken are proud to be partnering with Deliveroo again this year for their biggest Ramadan campaign to date. It's great to see Deliveroo supporting the Muslim community and we'll be offering limited edition Ramadan bundles to celebrate alongside our customers."

Zuneid Issa from Chilli Flames said, "Why not order our delicious family platters via Deliveroo for Iftar this Ramadan. Ramadhan Mubarak to all our Customers from the Chilli Flames family."

Kasim Akhtar Co Founder of Amigo's Burgers said, "Deliveroo always manages to deliver the best for Amigos. We are super excited for this Ramadan campaign, don't miss out on the great offers!"
