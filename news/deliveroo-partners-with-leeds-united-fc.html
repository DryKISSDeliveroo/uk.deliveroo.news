Deliveroo Scores Partnership With Leeds United

  • 7 August 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
delivery food football
Share this article

Leeds United are delighted to announce an exciting new partnership with online food delivery giants Deliveroo, which is the first of its kind within the football industry.

The innovative new partnership forms part of the Club's ongoing plans to continually improve the quality and variety of the matchday experience for our fans at Elland Road. This new partnership will allow guests within the Bremner Suite to order food from their favourite high street restaurants before the game and have it freshly made and delivered directly to the dedicated drop off point within the Suite.

Guests will be able to order food from a choice of 10 popular high street restaurants including Five Guys, KFC, Wagamama, Wok&Go, TGI Friday's, Prezzo, Krispy Kreme, Pizza Express, Frankie & Benny's and Chiquitos.

Executive Director Paul Bell said, "Deliveroo have quickly become one of the most talked about brands in the UK and we are delighted to announce this innovative new partnership with them. We aim to be forward thinking when it comes to the variety of choice and service we provide for our supporters and we feel that launching this new initiative will bring the high street to Elland Road and give our Bremner Suite guests an enhanced experience at home games. Through the partnership we will also work with Deliveroo to grow their customer base with our fans across the UK".

Dan Warne, UK MD at Deliveroo said: "We've been in Leeds since 2015 and it's host to one of our first ever Deliveroo Editions super kitchens. Leeds has an amazing food scene and we're thrilled to be able to open this up to fans at Elland Road".

Supporters can download the Deliveroo App at the App store and start to enjoy the food they love delivered by Deliveroo, with orders arriving in under 30 mins.

For more information on Deliveroo please visit their website at www.deliveroo.co.uk
