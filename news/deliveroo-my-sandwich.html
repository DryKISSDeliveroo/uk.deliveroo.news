THE ONE WHERE DELIVEROO RECREATES THE STOLEN SANDWICH

  • 10 October 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo sandwich office
Share this article
  • Deliveroo has recreated the ultimate office sandwich called "MY SANDWICH!!" for just £1 in London, Manchester & Brighton
  • Launched in time for offices re-opening, the turkey sandwich - complete with a gravy soaked moist making middle layer - will be available from Deliveroo Editions across London, Manchester and Brighton
  • It will have colleagues drooling with envy trying to steal it, and to protect it, sandwiches will come with a "MY SANDWICH!!" post it note

With Brits heading back to the office where they will once again experience microwave queues and wilted salads, Deliveroo has recreated the ultimate sandwich with a gravy soaked moist making middle layer that is so good, it is at risk of being stolen...

Priced at just £1.00, the launch of the "MY SANDWICH!!" comes as Deliveroo wants to make desk lunches more enjoyable for those heading back into the office, thanks to the triple-layered, delicious meal.

Office fridges have always been a hot-spot for lunch lifting, so to prevent this mouth-watering lunch-in-the-office sandwich from being stolen, Deliveroo has pre-printed a post-it on the compostable packaging that reads "MY SANDWICH!!" to help deter any colleagues from swiping the sandwich.

The sandwich is made with seeded granary bread, slow-cooked roasted turkey, homemade garlic and herb mayonnaise, sweet potato, cranberry sauce, crispy onions for added crunch, sage and onion stuffing, salad leaves and, of course, homemade gravy soaked into the middle bread layer of the sandwich.

For all the vegans out there, Deliveroo has cooked up an equally delicious plant-based alternative, packed with a delicious meat alternative, a perfectly whipped vegan mayo and a plant-based gravy-drenched middle layer.

Deliveroo spokesperson Aisha Jefferson, said, "Those heading back to the office will be able to get their hands on a sandwich that's good enough to steal as we've recreated an office sandwich so famously delicious, colleagues will be reaching into the communal fridge to swipe it. But fear not, thanks to a special post-it note on every item saying "MY SANDWICH!!", work mates won't go near it. Available to order in London, Manchester or Brighton, one of our riders will deliver it fresh to your office door or at home for just £1. Such a steal."

Deliveroo has thousands of amazing lunchtime options available for customers - from salads to sushi - delivered in under 30 minutes.

The limited edition sandwich is set to go on sale at 12pm on Tuesday 3rd August and will be available to Deliveroo customers across London, Manchester and Brighton until sold out. Search "Deliveroo's MY SANDWICH!!!" to find the store on the app.

Customers can place orders directly through the Deliveroo app, or online. Download the app here. App link will direct people to their device's app store.

About Deliveroo

Deliveroo is an award-winning delivery service founded in 2013 by William Shu and Greg Orlowski. Deliveroo works with over 115,000 best-loved restaurants and grocery partners, as well as 100,000 riders to provide the best food delivery experience in the world.  Deliveroo is headquartered in London, with around 2,000 employees in offices around the globe.

 Deliveroo operates in nearly 800 towns and cities across 12 markets, including Australia, Belgium, France, Hong Kong, Italy, Ireland, Netherlands, Singapore, Spain, United Arab Emirates, Kuwait and the United Kingdom.
