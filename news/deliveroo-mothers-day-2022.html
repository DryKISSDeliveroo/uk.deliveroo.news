DELIVEROO TO THE RESCUE: LAST MINUTE MOTHER’S DAY GIFTS DELIVERED IN AS LITTLE AS 20 MINUTES

  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo mothers day gifts last minute

  • Deliveroo customers can order last minute Mother's Day gifts on the app
  • Waitrose is offering a whopping 33% off their best-selling Prosecco through Deliveroo this Mother's Day
  • Flowers, beauty products, chocolates and cards delivered in as little as 20 minutes, through Deliveroo's partners like Boots, Sainsbury's, BP, Morrisons and Waitrose
  • Last year, sales of Mother's Day gifts increased by over  80% on Deliveroo in the run up
  • Flowers were the most ordered 'last minute' gift item on Deliveroo on Mother's Day 2021

23rd March, UK. Whether it's breakfast in bed or a present to unwrap in the morning, Deliveroo has everything that sons and daughters need to treat their mum on Mothering Sunday (27th March 2022). Delivered in as little as 20 minutes, Deliveroo offers a range of 'last minute' gift items, from flowers and beauty sets, to chocolates and more, that can be delivered straight to mum's front door

Gin, Prosecco and Champagne

Whether it's a glass of prosecco with breakfast or a gin and tonic in the garden post afternoon tea, Brits can treat their mum to a range of gins, proseccos and champagnes on-demand with Deliveroo. From this Friday, Waitrose is offering their best-selling Prosecco at a whopping 33% discount for just £7.99 (usually £11.95). Customers can also get their hands on M&S Prosecco and Rose £11 through BP.

Customers can also order their mum's favourite tipple like Tanqueray London Dry Gin, Taittinger Brut Reserve NV from Waitrose to Edinburgh Rhubarb & Ginger Gin from BP.

Flowers

Nothing says "I love you" like a bouquet of fresh flowers.  Give mum her favourite flowers through Deliveroo's grocery partners, which offer a wide selection of flowers to choose from, including:

  • Sainsbury's Indulgence Bouquet, £12.50
  • Waitrose Special Bouquet, £10
  • Waitrose Rose & Lily Bouquet, £12.50
  • Waitrose Mothers Day Carnation Bouquet. £4.00

On Mother's Day last year, flowers were the most ordered 'last minute' gift item on the Deliveroo app.

Breakfast

Make mum's morning with a luxe breakfast in bed - the ultimate start to Mother's Day, or treat her to a lie-in followed by delicious brunch to enjoy.  Whatever her favourite breakfast meal is, Deliveroo has a range of food items to cater for the occasion, all available with on-demand delivery.  

What's more, Whole Foods is offering a Mother's Day Brunch bundle. on the Deliveroo app, where customers can get their hands on a range of breakfast and brunch items, from croissants, pancakes and chocolate strawberries, to bagels, smoked salmon and ''macaroons - all available to order at the swipe of the app.

Beauty

Give mum the opportunity to feel relaxed and rested this Mother's Day by gifting her a range of beauty products, so she can be pampered like the Queen she is. For a touch of luxury, what's better than a long hot bath on Mother's Day?

Deliveroo customers can order products like the Neal's Yard Seaweed & Arnica Bath from Waitrose, or a range of Indulgent Bathing products from multiple Boots stores via Deliveroo, like the Ted Baker Little Beauties Body Care Gift Set Violet and Bergamot or the Sanctuary Spa Your Mini Moment Gift Set.  Bliss.

Chocolate

Show your appreciation for mum by ordering her favourite chocolates on Deliveroo this Mother's Day.  A perfect go-to gift that keeps on giving, no matter how many you eat.

Deliveroo's grocery partners offer a wide variety of irresistible chocolates from Ferrero Rochers, Lindor Assorted Boxes, M&S Truffles and more.  

A spokesperson for Deliveroo said, "Last year, in the run up, we saw an 80% increase in orders of gifts for Mothers Day, so we wanted to let customers know that we have their back with last-minute gift buying for mum this year too.  

Customers can have the confidence that they can rely on Deliveroo to get a gift to mum's door in as little as 20 minutes - and brighten her day with a smile.  We wish mums across the UK a very happy Mother's Day."

Customers can order on Deliveroo today and place orders directly through the Deliveroo app, or online. Download the app here. App link will direct people to their device's app store.
