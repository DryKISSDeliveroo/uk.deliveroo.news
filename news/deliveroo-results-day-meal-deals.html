ROOSULTS DAY! DELIVEROO IS OFFERING MONEY-SAVING MEAL DEALS TO CELEBRATE RESULTS DAY - WITH UP TO £16.17 & 25% OFF UP FOR GRABS

  • 24 August 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo results day family meal deals
Share this article
  • To celebrate Results Day, Deliveroo is offering money saving meal deals from nearly 4,000 restaurants, including PizzaExpress, Giggling Squid, Zizzi, Ask Italian, Frankie & Benny's and more
  • Families can save up to 25% off on meals deals from participating restaurants
  • Save £16.17 (27%) by purchasing Zizzi's Feed Four Bundle for just £59.90 instead of paying over £75!

16th August, UK: To celebrate A Level Results Day this Thursday (18th August), food delivery company, Deliveroo, is offering customers delicious money-saving bundles and meal deals of up to 25% off at nearly 4,000 restaurants.

Whether someone is collecting A-Levels, GCSE's, Juniors or Leaving certificates, Highers or National 5's, both families and friends can celebrate by ordering from customer favourite restaurants on Deliveroo including PizzaExpress (saving £9.79), Zizzi (saving £16.17), Frankie & Benny's (saving £6) and Joe & The Juice (saving, £5.20).

The offers will also be running for the remainder of the summer holidays and are available to all Deliveroo customers until the end of August, with some meal deals and money saving bundles offering up to 25% savings or meals for four people.

Deliveroo's Results Day money saving bundles and meal deals include:

  1. PizzaExpress** - Family Treats, £39.16 (usually £48.95) - saving £9.79

Pick 4 pizzas from any of PizzaExpress's classic or gluten free range and 4 soft drinks for £39.16 (usually £48.95).

Customers can also upgrade to bigger, thinner Romana bases, or load their pizzas with even more toppings for fuller flavour.

And that's not all; over 80 PizzaExpress** restaurants are offering many alternative mouth-watering deals on Deliveroo, including the 'Family Treat', which includes 3 x Margherita or American Pizzas & 1 x Dough Balls Doppio for just £31.56 (usually £39.45) - saving £7.89

  1. Giggling Squid - Summer Sizzle Family Bundle for Four, £49.95

Families who fancy a Thai feast can order from Giggling Squid on Deliveroo, where they can get dinner for 2 adults and 2 squidlets (veggie options available)

The bundle includes:

  • The choice of two starters (like Salt and Pepper Squid or the Delicious Thai Chicken Wings)
  • Two mains (like the Sticky Chicken or Green Curry),
  • Two kids tapas, which can range from Massaman Curry with Rice to Pad Thai (Chicken or Veggie)

  All for £49.95.

  1. Zizzi - Feed Four, £59.90 (saving 27%) - (usually costing up to £76.07) - saving £16.17

Zizzi's Feed Four bundle includes four mains, with choices from Zizzi's Rustica Pizzas, pastas or salads, as well as four best-selling sides for the price of £59.90, saving Deliveroo customers up to 27%.

  1. Joe & The Juice - Meal Deal for Four (usually £25.20) - saving £5.20

If families are looking for a lunchtime offer, they can head to Joe & The Juice on Deliveroo.

The deal includes:

  • Choose four from any of their iconic sandwiches like the Spicy Tuna Sandwich or the Avocado Sandwich, for the price of £20 (usually £25.20).
  1. Frankie & Benny's - Meal Deal for Four, £24 (usually £30) - saving £6

Frankie and Benny's Family Meal Deal for Four includes a choice of three pastas, like Spaghetti Carbonara or Rigatoni Arrabbiata, one 10" pizza like Pepperoni or BBQ Brisket and one side dish, all for £24 (usually £30).

  1. ASK Italian - Food for Four Bundle, £59

Families and friends looking for an Italian feast can feed four for dinner from Ask Italian.

The bundle includes:

  • Four starters (like N'Duja & Cheese Dough Bites or the Chicken Lecca-Lecca)
  • Four mains (like Linguine Carbonara, Al Forno Lasagne, Vegan Spaghetti Lentil Ragu, pizzas and so much more).

A Deliveroo spokesperson said, "After months of revision and exams, results day has finally arrived! We've made it easy for families & friends to celebrate exam achievements at home together; whether it's an Italian feast, tasty Thai or delicious pizza, they can bag the best deals and bundles at the click of a button for a takeaway night with Deliveroo."

Customers can search for the 'Family Meal Deals' tag on Deliveroo and place orders directly through the Deliveroo app, or online. Download the app here. App link will direct people to their device's app store.
