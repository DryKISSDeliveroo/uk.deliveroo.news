Greggs partners with deliveroo to trial delivery in London, Bristol & Newcastle

  • 13 November 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
deliveroo greggs
Share this article

Following the success of a recent trial in Birmingham, Greggs has launched a new delivery service trial with Britain's favourite food delivery service, Deliveroo, in London, Bristol and Newcastle.

The new service, available from three Greggs' shops, is now live and available exclusively through the Deliveroo app and website.

The Greggs menu on Deliveroo will feature a selection of popular menu items including: breakfast, freshly ground coffee, savouries and sausage rolls, freshly-prepared sandwiches, salads and sweet treats along with various meal deal options.

Ordering Greggs on Deliveroo is easy:

1.   Download the Deliveroo app or order online at www.deliveroo.co.uk

2.   Pick a delivery location - enter the address where you'd like your Greggs delivered to.

3.   Select one of the participating Greggs locations in the app.

4.   Place your order - pick the items you want right now or schedule your order to arrive when you want. The order will be charged to the card registered to your account.

5.   Customers can follow the progress of the delivery - get updates on the preparation of your order and watch your courier arrive live in-app.

Roger Whiteside, Chief Executive at Greggs, said: "When it comes to food-on-the-go, our customers tell us that convenience is key. We've been working hard to make Greggs available to our customers when and where they want it, whether that be in offices, industrial estates, retail parks, travel hubs and at home.

"We've been working on our delivery services over the past year and we're excited to launch a Deliveroo trial for our customers in London, Newcastle and Bristol. This will enable customers to enjoy our great tasting, good value food wherever they are, offering them the ultimate convenience."

Joe Groves at Deliveroo, added: "Gregg's is an icon of the British high street, we know Deliveroo customers are going to love getting their favourite bakes and bites delivered to them in under 30 mins."

Greggs' shops available for Deliveroo delivery include:

  • 243 Whitechapel Road, London, E1 1DB
  • 143 Strand, Central London, WC2R 1JA
  • 164 Buckingham Palace Road, London    SW1W 9TP
  • 78 East Street, Bedminster, Bristol    BS3 4JD
  • 87 Broadmead, Bristol    BS1 3DT
  • 209 Chillingham Road, Newcastle Upon Tyne    NE6 5LL
  • 42 Neville Street/Pink Lane, Newcastle    NE1 5DF
