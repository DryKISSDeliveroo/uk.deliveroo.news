NATIONAL PIZZA DAY: DELIVEROO LAUNCH UP TO 50% OFF CUSTOMER FAVOURITE PIZZAS

  • 29 March 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 5 min
deliveroo pizza day
Share this article
  • Deliveroo kicks off National Pizza Day celebrations by launching tasty discounts on the most popular pizzas available on the platform
  • Favourites include Pizza Express, Frankie & Bennys, Prezzo, Papa Johns, Pizza Pilgrims, Bella Italia and Cinquecento Pizzeria
  • From savings of up to 50% off, Deliveroo is offering great value deals this Pizza Day
  • New-to-Deliveroo customers can get £10 off their first delivery using the code FOODNOW10.**

7th February, UK: Slicers at the ready; Deliveroo has rounded up the best money-saving deals available on the platform to help Brits celebrate National Pizza Day (9th February) - a day designed to celebrate Italy's culinary icons and one of the most popular dishes on Deliveroo.

From Pizza Express's top rated pizza knocked by half of the price and 2 for £10 on pizzas at Bella Italia, to 20% off favourites like Cinquecento Pizzeria and Pizza Pilgrims and over 25% off Prezzo and Papa Johns, pizza fans should head over to the Deliveroo app if they want to get a slice of the money-saving action.

Pizza-loving Deliveroo customers will a-dough these offers, in particular Brighton locals as Deliveroo data has revealed they ordered the most pizza in the last 3 months.

Top 5 cities that order pizza on Deliveroo:

  1. Brighton
  2. London
  3. Manchester
  4. Birmingham
  5. Glasgow

A spokesperson at Deliveroo said, "Over the years, pizza has consistently been one of our customers' favourite dishes across the nation, but when it comes to toppings, we see a great variation in popularity and trends. Recent Deliveroo order data reveals our customers have a growing tolerance for the hot stuff, with orders of spicy pizzas like the Diavola and the Salamino Piccante trending in recent months.

Whatever your favourite topping, we're giving our customers exactly what they knead this National Pizza Day - the hottest and best value offers around from some of the best pizza joints in the UK, all delivered to your doorstep by Deliveroo."

  1. Pizza Express - 50% off one of their top rated pizzas, The American Hot

The fan favourite Pepperoni and Hot Pepper pizza is being slashed by half of the price this National Pizza Day. Piled with pepperoni, topped with mozzarella and with a choice of either hot green, hot & sweet chilli or jalapeño peppers, customers should head to the Deliveroo app to enjoy a spicy saving from Pizza Express's top rated pizza.

  1. Bella Italia - 2 for £10 on all pizzas

Bella Italia offers a huge variety of pizzas through Deliveroo. From classic pizzas like Prosciutto Bufala, Formaggio Funghi and Diavola, to a range of Pinsa Romana Pizza's (the most ancient form of Roman pizza made using rice flour mixed with other flour) and create your own.

Whatever the choice, and whether it be in a pair, with their families or on their own, Brits can tuck into two pizzas for just £10.

  1. Pizza Pilgrims -  20% off on Meal Deals

After selling their first Neapolitan pizza, with handmade, 48-hour proved dough and ingredients shipped in from Naples on Berwick St Market in 2011, Pizza Pilgrims have traded at a huge variety of events across the country and opened 18 pizzerias in London and Oxford, so it's no surprise that they are on Deliveroo's list.

Pizza Pilgrims have a selection of meal deals and customers can choose their favourite and enjoy an extra 20% off the price.

  • Hot Pizza and Cold Beer Deal (usually £35)
  • Any 2 pizzas, 3 crust dippers, garlic bread & 4 cans of beer
  • Pizza Club Del Vino (usually £40)
  • Any 2 pizzas, 3 crust dippers, garlic bread & a bottle of wine
  1. Papa Johns - Spend £30, save 30% on this special day

Since their first pizza which was made in Jeffersonville in Indiana, Papa Johns is available in 45 countries and territories around the world and Brits can get their pizzas delivered to their doors via Deliveroo.

In celebration of National Pizza Day, Deliveroo customers can get their hands on the high street favourite pizza for a whopping 30% off when they spend £30.

Only delivered by Deliveroo.

  1. Cinquecento Pizzeria - 20% off various pizzas

If customers are looking for authentic Neapolitan pizza that's "just like nonna used to make", but at a discount, then ''this is the one for them.

From classics like the Margherita, pistachio pesto and mortadella topped pizzas like the Romagnola, or spicy chilli pizzas like the Piccante, for just a few days only Cinquecento Pizzeria is offering a range of delicious pizzas for almost a quarter off the price.

Only delivered by Deliveroo.

  1. Prezzo - 25% off pizzas until the 11th February

One of the most well-known Italian dining restaurants on the high street, Prezzo, has a huge variety of pizzas available to order through Deliveroo.

Whether it be calzones or classics, pizza fans can get a whopping 25% off all pizzas from Prezzo.

Frankie & Bennys - 20% off

In celebration of the national day, the American Italian family favourite is knocking off nearly a quarter of the price of their entire menu. Customers can tuck into a range of dishes from Frankie & Benny's with 20% off, for a limited time only.

New customers can sign up to Deliveroo from today and get £10 off their first delivery when they spend a minimum of £15, using the code FOODNOW10.**

Customers can order great value pizzas and more on Deliveroo today and place orders directly through the Deliveroo app, or online. Download the app here. App link will direct people to their device's app store.
