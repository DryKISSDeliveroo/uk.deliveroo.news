THE BEST BURGERS AND DEALS EXCLUSIVELY ON DELIVEROO FOR NATIONAL BURGER DAY

  • 24 August 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 6 min
deliveroo national burger day
Share this article
  • Deliveroo is kicking off National Burger Day celebrations early by revealing a round-up of the best burgers, limited-edition specials and savings available on the platform
  • Favourites include GBK, Bleeker, Byron, Five Guys, Slim Chickens and Patty & Bun
  • From delicious discounts saving customers 20% off to meal deals with savings of up to £10.80, Deliveroo is offering various deals and promos this National Burger Day

Tuesday 23rd August: Deliveroo has rounded up the best mouth-watering, limited-edition burgers and money-saving deals available on the food-delivery app to help Brits celebrate National Burger Day (Thursday 25th August) - a day to celebrate one of the world's culinary icons and reigning title of the most popular dish on Deliveroo.

A variety of meal deals will be available to order on demand, from chicken sandwich meals saving customers up to £10.80 at Slim Chickens, burger and fries combos at Byron for £13.99 (usually £16.20), a whopping GBK burger deal for two for £22 (usually from £25.90 - £28.90) and at Patty & Bun, customers can save 20% off the entire menu for one day only. Bleecker and Patty & Bun are also offering limited-edition specials, so customers should head over to the Deliveroo app for the ultimate National Burger Day line up.

Arabella Jenkins at Deliveroo said, "If you want to celebrate National Burger Day without leaving your sofa (or breaking the bank!), then look no further. In order to treat Deliveroo customers and celebrate one of the most important days of the year, we decided to round up the most un-burger-leaveabe limited-edition burgers and money-saving deals from the some of the best burger joints in the UK."

  1. Patty & Bun -
  • National Burger Day special: The Galician
  • 20% off the entire menu on National Burger Day

A Deliveroo favourite, Patty & Bun has launched a limited-edition special in celebration of National Burger Day - The Galician. Complete with Rubia Gallega x Frissona Galician Beef Patty, truffle mayo, double red leicester cheese, pickles and onion, it's one that's not to be missed. Limited edition and available until 31st August.

Patty and Bun are also offering a whopping 20% off their entire menu when you spend £15, for one day only (26th August).

  1. GBK - Two burgers and two fries for £22 (usually varying from £25.90 - £28.90 SAVING at least £3.90)

Back in 2001, three Kiwi guys including world-renowned chef Peter Gordon set up the first Gourmet Burger Kitchen in Battersea, South London.

Not only do GBK have a range of burgers from 100% British beef burgers, classics, spicy or loaded and more to mouthwatering chicken burgers and vegan burgers but they also are renowned for their homemade sauces and mind-meltingly good milkshakes.

This National Burger Day, Deliveroo customers can get their hands on two classic burgers and two fries for £22 for GBK.

  1. Byron - 'Byron and fries combo' for £13.99 (usually £16.20 AND SAVING £2.21)

Another must-try burger joint in the eyes of Deliveroo, Byron. Founded by Tom Byng in 2007 with a vision to keep hamburgers as classic as they should be, Byron now has over 50 restaurants across the UK

In celebration of the classic beef burger (because sometimes you just can't beat a classic, right?) Deliveroo customers can get their hands on the 'Byron Burger' and fries for just £13.99 (saving £2.21). The Byron is made with 100% Angus Aberdeen 6oz beef patty, American cheese, smoked streaky bacon, signature Byron sauce, lettuce, homemade pickled red onion and pickles.

Only delivered by Deliveroo.

  1. Bleecker - Exclusive National Burger Day special: The Pimento Cheeseburger

Bleeker is offering a National Burger Day special, The Pimento Cheeseburger. A favourite in the state of South Carolina, the Pimento Cheeseburger is made from cheese, mayo, hot sauce and pimento peppers.

Customers can order The Pimento Cheeseburger on Deliveroo for £12, but they better hurry! The National Burger Day special is a limited edition, available for one day only (25th) and while stocks last.

Only delivered by Deliveroo.

  1. Slim Chickens - Money-saving Meal Deals:
  • Any two chicken sandwich meals for £22  (individual meals usually £11.50 - £13.50 each, SAVING up to £5.40)
  • Any four chicken sandwich meals for £40 (individual meals usually £11.50 - £13.50 each, SAVING up to £10.80)

''Famous for its chicken tenders, Slim Chickens offers good old Southern American experience, serving up cooked-to-order premium chicken. Diners can choose from Slims' signature buttermilk marinated chicken and house specialities, including crispy tenders, alongside a range of 15 signature dipping sauces including Blue Cheese, Sriracha Garlic and Fiery Inferno - but be warned: it's seriously hot!

This National Burger Day, Deliveroo customers can get their hands on a choice of two discounted 100% all natural chicken sandwiches meal deals:

  1. Any two chicken sandwich meals for £22
  2. Any four chicken sandwich meals for £40

Each meal comes with a chicken sandwich, regular fries, soft drink, and a choice of Slim Chickens dipping sauces.

  1. Five Guys - Deliveroo recommends

Loved by the nation and only delivered by  Deliveroo, Five Guys is an iconic burger joint providing premium quality and fresh ingredients, with popular dishes including Bacon Cheeseburgers, Cajun Fries and Peanut Butter Shakes. Customers across the UK will be able to order from the participating Five Guys restaurants via Deliveroo.

A must try for National Burger Day? None other than the Five Guys Cheeseburger! A classic burger which continually makes it to the top most ordered dishes on Deliveroo every year, made up of two-patties with two slices of melted American cheese - iconic.

.

  1. Hooyah Burger -
  • Deliveroo Exclusive National Burger Day Special: Leeds Rhinos
  • 50% off the exclusive burger (usually £10.99, SAVING £5.50)

Deliveroo and Leeds local burger restaurant, Hooyah Burgers, have teamed up to launch a limited edition burger to honour Leeds Rhinos, as part of Deliveroo's partnership with the professional Rugby League team.  Local customers can also get 50% off the exclusive Leeds Rhinos burger on National Burger Day (usually £10.99, saving £5.50).

The Deliveroo exclusive burger, which will be available from 25th August until the 28th August, has been 'try-ed' and tasted' by Leeds Rhinos men's first team players including, Tom Holroyd, James Donaldson, Liam Tindall and rising star Max Simpson - who've given it their official 'rhinos hoof' of approval ahead of its launch on National Burger Day in Leeds.
