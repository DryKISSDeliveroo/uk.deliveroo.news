DELIVEROO CUSTOMERS ENJOY 14 DAYS OF FREE DELIVERY ON GROCERIES AND ESSENTIALS THIS TWIXMAS

  • 20 December 2021
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo twixmas free delivery
Share this article
  • Deliveroo is offering FREE delivery for 14 days for existing customers on grocery orders from boxing day to the new year*
  • Customers can enjoy FREE delivery on party food and drinks for New Years Eve like prosecco and canapes, delivered in as little as 20 minutes
  • A selection of money-saving deals on products for New Years Eve, such as wines and spirits will be available through Co-op, One Stop and BP on Deliveroo

December, UK: 'Twixmas' - those all important relaxation days between Christmas and New Year's Eve - is a time for Brits to put their feet up, pop their PJ's on and recharge at home. That's why Deliveroo has announced its providing FREE delivery for existing customers on their groceries and essentials from Boxing Day to the New Year*, so that Brits can stay stocked up without having to leave the house.

With supermarkets and grocers including Waitrose, Morrisons, Aldi, Sainsbury's, Co-op, Morrisons, BP, One Stop, Londis and more all on Deliveroo, customers across the UK can get essentials like bread, eggs, milk and vegetables as well as non-food items like toilet paper and shampoo, delivered to their door in under 20 minutes with no delivery charge.

For those preparing for New Years Eve celebrations at home, Deliveroo also has a range of New Years Eve party essentials on offer. On top FREE delivery, here are some of the extra grocery and essentials discounts that Deliveroo has to help customers save after Christmas:

  • SPIRITS: Whether it's gin or whisky, rum or vodka, customers can get a range of 70CL spirits for just £16.95 from BP, knocking £5 off the usual price.
  • BAILEY'S: Those that love to enjoy a Bailey's, whether it's straight over ice or in a hot chocolate, can get The Original Irish Cream for just £15 from both BP and One Stop, saving nearly £7.
  • WINE: Co-op is offering a range of wines also at a discounted price. Customers can get their hands on red wines like Villa Garducci Merlot and Hereford Red Tempranillo or whites like Las Moras Pinot Grigio and Eagle's Pass Chardonnay for just £5.

A spokesperson from Deliveroo said, "Whether you're relaxing, spending time with family and friends or already up and about preparing for New Years Eve, Deliveroo is available to deliver everything you need in as little as 20 minutes over the Twixmas period, and what is more, you can get these essentials delivered for free! With a range of great deals and offers on New Year's party essentials, you won't even need to leave the house until the party starts."

Here are the steps on how to get 14 days FREE delivery on essentials via Deliveroo (for existing customers only):

  1. Customers need to place their first grocery order between 26th December and 1st January through the Deliveroo app.
  2. 14 days of free delivery will be automatically applied to all grocery orders for 14 days from when the customer places the first order

Customers can order food on Deliveroo today and place orders directly through the Deliveroo app, or online. New customers can download the app here. App link will direct people to their device's app store.

Deliveroo's opening hours will continue as normal throughout the Twixmas period**
