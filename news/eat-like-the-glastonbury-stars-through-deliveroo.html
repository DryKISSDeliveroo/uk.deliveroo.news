Eat Like Ed Sheeran at Glastonbury!

  • 20 June 2017
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
glastonbury food
Share this article

We are offering backstage 'riders' of this year's Glasto performers

  • 109 items make up the backstage 'rider' requests of this year's Glastonbury headliners.
  • The Foo Fighters are the most demanding, with 92 items requested, followed by Katy Perry and Ed Sheeran.
  • These Rockstar 'riders' will be available to order through the Deliveroo app from 21st – 26th June.

Glastonbury tickets sell out in seconds. There is a good chance you didn't get one, but that doesn't mean you should have to miss out on all of the fun. Deliveroo has today unveiled its Rockstar 'riders', allowing you to live like a rock star and order the food demanded by the stars performing at this year's festival.

The packages will offer food identical to that demanded by the Foo Fighters, Katy Perry and Ed Sheeran in their dressing rooms, before taking to the stage. Whilst a Mariah Carey like basket of puppies, white linen drapes or blue smarties don't feature; these stars certainly have their own unique set of asks.

Foo Fighters frontman, Dave Grohl has an Everlong package; made up of ninety-two items, including 65% cocoa chocolate bars, pomegranate juice, crunchy peanut butter and jam with over thirty-one pieces of fruit to keep a healthy balance. This rock stars 'rider' will set you back a grand total of £145.10.

Equally, you can chow down like California girl, and pop diva Katy Perry with some slightly more up-market demands, including a bottle of Pinot Grigio, dried apples, and some freeze-dried strawberries. These mixtures of fresh and fancy delights for one costs just £21.90.

Alternatively, eat like Ed and his A-team for £57.31, by enjoying some down to earth Robinson's squash, Fanta, and a jar of manuka honey to sooth those sore vocal chords before singing along with the TV.

Each backstage 'rider' will be available to order online and through the app, simply select 'Rockstar Requests' but you will have to be fast, as stock is limited!
