Deliveroo reveals 100 most popular dishes of 2019

  • 12 December 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 7 min
deliveroo deliveroo100 foodtrends
Share this article

As the year comes to a close, food delivery service Deliveroo takes a moment to reflect on the food highlights of 2019 and reveal the most popular dishes around the world, as chosen by its customers. From bibimbap to burritos - this year's eclectic mix of take-away favourites certainly makes for an interesting read.

Here's a taster of this year's top ten dishes on Deliveroo around the world:

Deliveroo's top ten most-ordered dishes 2019

  1. Poké Signature Super Protéiné from Pokawa, Paris
  2. Cheeseburger from Five Guys, London
  3. Burrito Pollo from Gonzalez & Co, Barcelona
  4. Salmon Pokè from Pokèria by NIMA, Milan
  5. Footlong Sub from Subway, Dublin
  6. Tommy the Chook from Boss Burger Co, Geelong
  7. 米線+一餸 from 譚仔雲南米線 Tam's Shop, Hong Kong
  8. Shack Burger from Shake Shack,        Dubai
  9. Big Giant Menu from Quick, Liege
  10. Sushi Burrito from Sushito, Amsterdam

It turns out that Hawaiian poke bowls are the real stars of the show in 2019. The incredibly instagrammable and equally nutritious Hawaiian dish has been revealed as a favourite in nine different countries and makes up a fifth of the global top 20 list.

Deliveroo also reports an increase in orders of more traditional takeaway cuisines and streetfood favourites including the Best Mountain Burger from Greg and Jerry's in Grenoble and a Gyros from Amsterdam's Meat & Greek.  

A closer look at Deliveroo's top 100 reveals some fun foodie ordering trends over the course of 2019.

Health-conscious

With orders of healthy options increasing by 51% this year it's no surprise dishes like the Kale Salad from Hong Kong's KALE or the Chicken Milanese from Simple Health Kitchen in London have made it into the top 100.

Crazy for chicken

Whether fried, grilled or rolled into a burrito, it's clear that people love chicken with poultry-based dishes filling a fifth of all the spots on this year's Deliveroo 100.  Favourites include Chicken Rice from Boon Tong Kee, Singapore, Grilled Boneless Chicken from Dubai's Al Mandaloun and the 9 Hotwings Boxmeal from Eindhoven's KFC.

Asian cuisine

This year, a quarter of those featured in the Deliveroo 100 were traditional Asian dishes. Favourites include Bibimbap Viande from Pierre Sang Express in Paris and Sushi roll di salmone e avocado from Basara, Milan.

Tasty traditions

This year's top 100 also showed that people are celebrating their local favourites. Favourite national dishes include Cod and Chips from Harbourside Fish & Chips in Plymouth, Regina Pizza from Milan's Assaje, and Beef Hor Fan from Rasa Rasa, Singapore.

About Deliveroo

Deliveroo is an award-winning delivery service founded in 2013 by William Shu and Greg Orlowski. Deliveroo works with over 80,000 best-loved restaurants and takeaways, as well as 60,000 riders to provide the best food delivery experience in the world.  Deliveroo is headquartered in London, with over 2,500 employees in offices around the globe.

 Deliveroo operates in over 500 towns and cities across 13 markets, including Australia, Belgium, France, Hong Kong, Italy, Ireland, Netherlands, Singapore, Spain, Taiwan, United Arab Emirates, Kuwait and the United Kingdom.
