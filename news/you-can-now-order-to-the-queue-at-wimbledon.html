Order Breakfast to the Queue at Wimbledon Tennis Tournament

  • 3 July 2017
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
wimbledon breakfast
Share this article
  • Deliveroo is launching a special breakfast delivery service so visitors of this year's Wimbledon tennis championships can order to the queue
  • Local London favourites including Carluccio's and Le Pain Quotidien have signed up offering tasty breakfast bites to tennis fans

Deliveroo has partnered with some of Wimbledon's favourite breakfast haunts to offer a breakfast delivery service to visitors of The Championships.

Le Pain Quotidien, Pret A Manger, Carluccio's, CAU, Waffle Jack's, Bill's, Crepeaffaire, Mr Pretzels, Cafe Rosi, Lu-Ma Cafe and Wafflemeister will all offer a breakfast menu, with delivery starting from 8am until 12pm.

Visitors of The Championships can use the Deliveroo site or app to order their breakfast any day throughout the tournament period, which will be cooked fresh to order and delivered straight to the queue by Deliveroo riders within 30 minutes. The pick-up point for all deliveries is Wimbledon Park Road Gate, where the entrance to the queue is located (see map here).

Caroline Hazlehurst, General Manager at Deliveroo, said: "We're extremely excited to be partnering with some of the finest breakfast eateries in Wimbledon to bring some of their delicious morning dishes to hungry Wimbledon goers!

"So whether you need a health boost to get you into the tennis spirit or you're craving a hearty breakfast before a day out in the sun, our riders will be at the queue within 30 minutes to satisfy your breakfast needs."
