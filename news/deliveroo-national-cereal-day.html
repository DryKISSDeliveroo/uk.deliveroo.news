SUSHI FLAVOURED CEREAL? CEREAL-SLY? DELIVEROO LAUNCHES LIMITED-EDITION TAKEAWAY FLAVOURED CEREAL RANGE

  • 29 March 2022
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo cereal day
Share this article
  • This National Cereal Day (7th March), Deliveroo launches a one-of-a-kind cereal range inspired by the UK's most loved takeaway dishes
  • Costing just £1 a box, limited edition DeliverOO's will be available in three flavours: Sushi Roll, Margarita Pizza and Chicken Katsu Curry
  • Customers in London, Bristol and Manchester can order 'DeliverOO's' from 7th March, while stocks last

This National Cereal Day (7th March) it's time to be bowl-d over your breakfast as Deliveroo launches a limited-edition range of takeaway flavoured cereal, including Sushi Roll, Margarita Pizza, and Chicken  Katsu Curry. The limited edition cereal range called DeliverOO's will be available from 7th of March giving customers the opportunity to enjoy their favourite takeaway meal with a splash of milk.

Tricking taste buds across the country, DeliverOO's is the perfect cereal treat for breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner. Or as some may say, 'brinner', as foodies and fans combine their favourite dinners with the UK's most loved breakfast option. DeliverOO's come in three limited-edition flavours which will leave customers asking, "Wheat did I just eat?".

  • For the first time, customers can enjoy Sushi Roll Flavoured DeliverOO's (pescetarian), seasoned with savoury seaweed powder and tangy pickled ginger, a perfect take on the East Asian staple (maybe leave the chopsticks in the draw for this one).  
  • Italian's might not believe it, but the Margherita Pizza Flavoured DeliverOO's (v) strikes a perfect balance of tangy cheese and punchy tomato, topped off with a hint of sweet dried black olives.
  • People with a breakfast craving for curry can pour themselves a bowl of Katsu Curry Flavoured DeliverOO's featuring keynotes of mild chilli powder, turmeric and coconut milk.

Deliveroo spokesperson said "When we heard it was World Cereal Day, we wanted to 'bowl' over cereal lovers with a breakfast dish to remember. We know our customers love to try weird and wonderful fusion dishes - which is why we've really milked it with this range of cereals. If you live in Manchester, Bristol or London, search 'DeliverOO's' in the app and for a bargain £1 a pack, you can take a break from cereal monogamy and try something deliciously different for breakfast this week."

Search 'DeliverOO's: Food Flavoured Cereal Shop' on the Deliveroo app, or online to place an order near you. Subject to geographical location and availability.
